📋 Project Overview

🧠

A deep learning-based traffic monitoring system designed to analyze road activity in real time using computer vision techniques.

🎯

Utilizes YOLOv8 to detect and classify vehicles such as cars, bikes, and trucks from live video streams.

📹

Processes input from CCTV cameras or webcams to continuously monitor traffic conditions.

🛣️

Applies lane segmentation to identify road lanes and structure.

🔢

Tracks vehicles across frames by assigning a unique ID to each object.

⚡

Estimates vehicle speed based on motion (pixel displacement over time).

📏

Computes distance between vehicles to assess traffic density and safety.

⚠️

Detects unsafe driving behavior and evaluates potential accident risks.

🔔

Generates real-time alerts (sound and email) for critical situations.

💾

Stores detected data and alerts in an SQLite database for analysis and reporting.
